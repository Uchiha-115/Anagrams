Welcome to the Naruto Character Anagram Game


Here are the rules of the game

1.A character name will appear Jumbled

2.Use the text box to put in your answer

3.Click the enter button to submit your answer

4.If correct the next name will appear and you'll get a point as well as 5 more seconds

5.If wrong you'll have to try again but you'll lose 5 seconds

6.The game is over when you guess all the names correctly or run out of time
